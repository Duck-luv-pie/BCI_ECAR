ABOUT US

Welcome to the Bluevale Electric Car Club, where our passion for electric vehicles drives us to not only create but also excel in the realm of sustainable mobility. Based in Waterloo, Ontario and competing at the esteemed University of Waterloo, our journey has been defined by an incredible track record of success and innovation.

As a team, we specialize in designing, building, and racing electric cars that have not only turned heads but also earned us a place among the elite in the world of competitive engineering. Our commitment to excellence has propelled us to become recurring champions in various competitions, and our trophy cabinet proudly showcases over 40 awards.

These accolades are not just tokens of achievement; they represent our dedication to pushing the boundaries of electric vehicle technology. Our victories have been hard-fought, our innovations groundbreaking, and our impact on the sustainable transportation landscape profound. From engineering breakthroughs to pioneering aerodynamics, our journey has been defined by a relentless pursuit of innovation.

At the core of our mission is a deep passion for shaping the future of mobility. We believe that electric vehicles are not just the future; they are the present, and we are leading the charge in making that future cleaner, greener, and more exciting. We invite you to join us on our journey as we continue to break barriers, drive innovation, and inspire change in the world of electric vehicles.

So, whether you're a fellow enthusiast, a curious onlooker, or someone who shares our vision for a sustainable future, we welcome you to explore our world at the Bluevale Electric Car Club. Together, we are electrifying the future, one race at a time, and the road ahead is full of excitement and possibilities.
